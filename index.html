Our Experience

Welcome to our page dedicated to our Erasmus experience in the beautiful Spanish village of
Obon! During our stay in this idyllic place, we had the privilege to explore not only the
wonderful landscapes of the regions, but also the deep concepts of ecology and gender equality.
With each sunrise and each conversation with our organizers, we discovered new perspectives and
learned valuable lessons about our relationship with the environment and the importance of
promoting gender equality. In this page, we will share our experiences, thoughts and 
reflections.

Our experience started at Marisica, the place where we stayed. It was a place full of charm
and peace. The hosts welcomed us with open arms, turning every moment into an experience full
of hospitality
